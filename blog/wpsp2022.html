Project Overview


The field work detailed in this blog post is occuring between January 11th, 2022 and February 23rd, 2022.


The research vessel (R/V) Kilo Moana is a 186′ Small Waterplane Area Twin Hull (SWATH) vessel owned by the U.S. Navy and operated by the University of Hawai‘i Marine Center. The ship acts as a multi-purpose oceanographic research vessel with extensive equipment for geophysical, physical oceanographic, meteorological, and radioisotope research.
